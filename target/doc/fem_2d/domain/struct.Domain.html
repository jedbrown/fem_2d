pub fn galerkin_sample_gep<SF, AI, BI>(
    &self,
    num_gauss_quad: Option<usize>
) -> GEP where
    SF: ShapeFn,
    AI: Integral,
    BI: Integral, 

Fill two system matrices using the domains it’s Basis Space as the Testing Space. Return a Generalized Eigenproblem (GEP)

All pairs of overlapping Shape Functions will be integrated and stored in the matrices by their associated DoF IDs

  • Two Integrals: AI and BI must be specified. These are used to populate the A and B matrices respectively
  • A ShapeFn SF must also be specified. This is used to evaluate the Domains BasisSpecs
